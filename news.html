My Apps
Editable Profile

November 17th, 2020

After login you can now edit your profile via the "Profile" entry in the user dropdown at the top right. There you can see your username and full name, your password can be changed and an URL to a profile picture can be entered.
Editable App Metadata

November 16th, 2020

The metadata of an app such as title, description and category can now also be edited later.
List of your own Apps

November 11th, 2020

After login you can now show all your created apps via the "My Apps" entry in the user dropdown at the top right. The list contains both the published apps and the unpublished apps.
Login, Logout and Registration

November 10th, 2020

At the top right there is now a user dropdown via which you can log in, log out and, for the first time, create a free account yourself.
Choose to Publish or not Publish an App

November 9th, 2020

You can now freely choose whether or not to publish a new app. Apps that have not been published are not listed and therefore cannot be found using the search function, but can still be shared with others via the app URL.
